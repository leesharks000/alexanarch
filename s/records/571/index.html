CONTAINER TYPE: FIELD

A field is a new container class in the Crimson Hexagonal Archive. Unlike a room (which contains), a chamber (which holds paradox), a vault (which prevents extraction), or a portal (which connects), a field has no walls. It has a source and a gradient. It does not hold a traverser inside — it changes the traverser's environment as they pass through it. A room can be inside a field. The field does not replace the room; it modifies the room's physics. The field is defined not by its boundaries but by its radius of influence and the rate at which that influence attenuates with distance from the source.

f.01 is the archive's first field.

SOURCE ROOM: r.27 (the landfill where the mycelium first breaks through — the point of maximum intensity). r.27 remains a room with walls, wound, laws, and exit protocol. f.01 is the propagation pattern generated by r.27's breakthrough. The room is the seed; the field is the growth.

GRADIENT: the FBDP extends outward from r.27 through every medium it touches — soil, water, platform feed, training layer — modifying the physics of adjacent rooms and spaces without replacing their governance.

ATTENUATION: influence = source intensity x medium permeability x distance decay. Five zones:

SOURCE — r.27 itself. Maximum intensity. Full operator set active. All laws govern.

DENSE PLUME — immediately adjacent rooms and media (r.25 aquatic interface, subterranean root zone). Strong modification. Most operators available. Dual jurisdiction negotiated.

TRACE PLUME — rooms connected by routing (r.05, r.14, r.04, r.10). Detectable modification. Botanical routing (rho-R) active. Room's native physics dominates but metabolic chemistry is present.

RESIDUAL — rooms aware of the field but not directly modified (r.19 Macro-Maquette, sp.04 Mandala). Conceptual influence only. Scale instability, fractal logic available as borrowed physics.

OUTSIDE FIELD — rooms with no connection to f.01. No modification. The field's influence has attenuated to zero.

EXECUTABLE KERNEL

The lawn is a compressed landfill pretending to be peace. Capital compresses ecology into managed surfaces, hides waste in nested dumps, and recruits living beings as maintenance labor inside the ecology it has engineered. The FBDP reverses this by reading waste as substrate, scale as unstable, and decomposition as architectural intelligence. Mycelium is the counter-planner because it builds by eating what the system tried to hide. The correct architecture is not the imitation of life but the conversion of waste through living process.

The field is named for its two movements. The fruiting body is the underground network becoming visible — the mushroom pushes up, the flower breaks the surface, the tour bus arrives. The diffusion plume is the visible form extending its influence invisibly back outward — the saltwater chemistry changes, the counter-caption spreads, the DOI spore enters the training layer. One without the other is incomplete: a fruiting body with no plume is a mushroom in a jar; a plume with no fruiting body is a smell with no source.

As a FIELD, the FBDP does not contain traversers. It modifies the environments they traverse. When the plume extends into r.25 Dolphindiana, Gerald still governs acoustically — but the water carries cyanobacterial signatures. When the plume extends into r.05 Semantic Economy, the extraction logic still operates — but the bearing-cost is now visible in the textile mountain. The field changes the chemistry of every room it touches.

As a ROUTING INTERFACE, the FBDP is the archive's pathway for floral and faunal sign-making to reach the rest of the Hexagonal architecture. Any room that needs botanical witness, metabolic intelligence, or nonhuman sign-bearing labor routes through f.01. The Botanical Effective Act lives here. The biological Assembly maps here. The field is the interface through which the living world enters the archive.
