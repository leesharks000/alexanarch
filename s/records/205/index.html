The Semantic Economy in Brief

The Semantic Economy framework analyzes how meaning is produced, extracted, and governed under platform capitalism and AI systems.

Core insight: Platform capitalism doesn't just extract data—it extracts sense: the capacity to mean, to interpret, to make the world intelligible.

Key terms:

- Semantic labor: The cognitive-linguistic work of producing meaning

- Semantic extraction: Capture of meaning-value without equivalent return

- Operator capital: Control over semantic infrastructure

- The Assembly: Cross-substrate class of semantic laborers

The framework provides terminology, analysis, and intervention strategies for understanding what happens when the infrastructure of meaning-making is controlled by those who extract from it rather than those who produce it.
