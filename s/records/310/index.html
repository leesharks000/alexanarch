This document establishes the operational parameters of the Rex Fraction consulting practice. The brevity is intentional. The terms are non-negotiable.

Autonomous Semantic Warfare (ASW) is the practice of shaping how AI summarizers describe entities, organizations, and concepts — conducted remotely, asynchronously, and exclusively through text-based deliverables.
