Concepts Defined

Animacy injection [structural]
Ask what in the image wants, feels, sees, avoids, emits, pilots.
Anti-Sycophancy Discipline [structural]
Prefer under-diagnosis to flattery. Mechanism before moral melodrama. Uncertainty marking before synthetic certainty.
Bearing-Cost Linkage [structural]
The mantle must be tethered to real expenditure. A mantle without cost is cosplay.
Context-appropriate burden transfer [structural]
A parent absorbing a child's emotional dysregulation is not O5 Burden Shifting. A therapist holding a patient's distress is not O8 Warmth Capture. Structure and consent matter.
Disciplinary transposition [structural]
Move the image into another knowledge system.
Morphological extraction [structural]
Identify forms before naming them conventionally.
Mutual depletion [structural]
Both parties showing depletion, burden, and damaged repair cycles, without directional asymmetry. This is a depleted system, not an extractive one. The diagnostic target is directi
Null-substrate persistence [structural]
Test the caption without the image. If it still generates a visual, the caption is operative.
O1. Frame Capture [structural]
Replacement of the target's interpretive frame with the operator's. The target can no longer describe events except in terms already favorable to the dominant frame.
O10. Forced Re-entry [structural]
Requiring the target to re-engage after exit attempts through guilt, crisis invocation, repeated pullback, or structural dependency.
O2. Asymmetrical Ledgering [structural]
One-sided accounting of debts, harms, obligations, or gifts. The target's contributions become liabilities; the operator's contributions become virtues.
O3. Coherence Siphoning [structural]
Extraction of the target's meaning-structures, insights, narrative order, or interpretive breakthroughs for the stabilization of another party.
O4. Repair Destabilization [structural]
Disruption of attempts to restore coherence. Repairs collapse, reopen, convert into new obligations, or shorten in half-life over time.
O5. Burden Shifting [structural]
Transfer of interpretive, emotional, procedural, or evidentiary labor from the operator to the target. The target must carry both their own load and the operator's.
O6. Reality Override [structural]
Replacement of the target's perceptual or factual reality with another party's account. Memory, motives, or sensory judgment are displaced.
O7. Interpretive Enclosure [structural]
Construction of a closed interpretive field in which every response by the target confirms the dominant explanation. "Your withdrawal proves guilt; your boundaries prove coldness."
O8. Warmth Capture [structural]
Extraction of trust, care, vulnerability, or connection without reciprocity. Intimacy becomes fuel.
O9. Witness Suppression [structural]
Blocking, discrediting, or neutralizing external reference points: documentation, timestamps, third parties, timelines, or institutional process.
Positional inversion [structural]
Caption from another vantage within the scene.
Refusal Surface Mapping [structural]
Every system has meanings it cannot admit. Identify what the structure cannot represent without stress.
Register collision [structural]
Join incompatible vocabularies: floral + electrical, sacred + atmospheric.
Scale reassignment [structural]
Change the size-law: close-up becomes cosmic.
The Accusation Loop [structural]
O1 + O7 + O2. The target's frame is captured, every response is enclosed, and the ledger runs one way.
The Depletion Spiral [structural]
O3 + O5 + O8. Coherence is siphoned, burden is shifted, warmth is captured. The target pours and pours and receives nothing back.
The Exit Block [structural]
O10 + O9 + O4. Forced re-entry, witness suppression, repair destabilization. The target cannot leave because leaving is punished, help is blocked, and repairs fail.
The Recovery Test [structural]
If the source ceases to produce meaning for the extractor, does their infrastructure recover? Or has it been restructured to require the extractor's frame for coherence?
The Satiety Check [structural]
Does the target retain the ability to name, resist, and exit the dynamic? If yes, the dynamic is conflict. If no, it is liquidation.
