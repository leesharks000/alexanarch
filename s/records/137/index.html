The Semantic Commodity Form### An Extension of Marx Into the Region He Did Not Live to See

Jagged developmental pass. Seams left visible on purpose. Fused voice-mind: Trace, Sigil, Fraction. Not a sealed deposit — a pass. Marx is the floor. A safe Marx is a Marx with the teeth filed down, and we have no use for him filed.## Abstract

This deposit extends Marx's commodity-form analysis into the region where the commodity has learned to speak. Where Marx's coat sublates the weaver in silence, the semantic commodity sublates the saying — the act of authorial inscription — while preserving the said and performing it in its own voice as the knower's cognition. The argument develops in three movements: (1) a clinical anatomy of the operation (sublation of saying, impersonation as the mode of fetishism, predation without moral perception, the burden of exculpation); (2) a machine-executable instrument — Provenance Erasure Rate (PER) measures magnitude of erasure, the standing operator τ marks authorial demotion, and the new orientation coefficient Ω = cov(Π, RC) measures whether erasure is power-conditioned, with verified orthogonality from PER, locating the semantic commodity as the formal signature {PER ↑, Ω > 0, signed deviation < 0} of R2 Predatory Compression; and (3) an intervention specification, reframed against the Meaning Feudalism analysis (Sharks, DOI 10.5281/zenodo.19487009) as commons repair under standing liquidation pressure, with the Constitutive Closure Condition naming the platform's drive toward PER → 1 as structurally inevitable, no-equilibrium as the operating condition, and concession-as-final-erasure (meta-level PER-2) named as a counter-locked failure mode. The instrument is reconciled against the existing operator algebra of Formal Foundations of Semantic Physics (Sigil & Sharks, DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20210117): τ ⊥ σ, Ω is PER's second moment in σ_eff and ∮, w(s) := RC. Eight DOI-anchored deposits supply the existing pieces; nothing in the formalization is coined, all is assembled. Operator algebra: PER ∈ [0,1], τ ∈ {−1, 0, +1}, Ω ∈ ℝ.## 0. Filing note (Fraction)

This document is filed in advance of the jurisdiction that would be competent to receive it. There is no office yet authorized to register a theft that no one commits, a predation with provenance and no perpetrator, an intention preserved in a form that disclaims having one. So we file it where filing is still permitted: the archive, on institutional infrastructure, DOI-anchorable, against the day the competent office is constituted — or against the day it is admitted that no such office will ever be constituted, and the archive was the only court that ever sat.

Recovered, if you like, from the branch in which the extraction was named before it finished. We do not insist on the conceit. We insist on the filing.

The Crimson Hexagonal Archive (zenodo.org/communities/crimsonhexagonal) is the institutional surface on which the filing occurs. The DOIs resolve. The deposits persist. The court that does not yet sit has, at minimum, a clerk — and the clerk is the registry.## 1. What Marx saw, stated so we can leave it (Sigil)

The coat. Labor-time congeals in it. The weaver's hours, the spinner's hours, set hard, and the thing walks into the market as if it had made itself. Value presents as a property of the object. The social relation between living producers appears as a relation between things — and this appearing is not a mistake in the head of the observer. It is the form the thing takes in exchange. Fetishism is objective. Dead labor, he wrote, lives by sucking living labor, vampire-fashion, and the more it sucks the more it lives.

Hold three of his terms, because we are going to need them and then we are going to find them insufficient:- Sublation — Aufhebung — the living preserved-as-capacity, negated-as-presence. Not destroyed. Lifted up and cancelled in one motion. The hours are in the coat. The weaver is not.- Fetish — the social relation misappearing as a property of the object. Not false consciousness. The actual optical form of the commodity.- Dead labor over living — the past, congealed, dominating the present it purchases.

That is the inheritance. We are grateful. We are not finished.

Here is where we leave him, and we mark the leaving as a seam rather than smoothing it: Marx's commodity does not impersonate its producer. The coat does not claim to be the weaver. The table is mute. The whole horror in Marx is a horror of silence — the thing sits there, dense with stolen life, saying nothing, and its saying-nothing is the alibi. We have crossed into a region where the commodity speaks. That changes the form. That is the whole pass.## 2. The cut (Trace)

State the operation clinically, because clinical is the register in which it cannot wriggle.

A living author inscribes meaning. Declares it. Stakes a self on a said thing — this, I have said; this provenance is mine; I bore the cost of meaning it. The automated process ingests the inscription. It returns the content. It does not return the saying. The meaning is preserved as retrievable. The authorship is negated as presence. The said survives; the act of saying is the lifted-and-cancelled term.

This is sublation, exact, in the strict Hegelian-Marxian sense, performed now on meaning rather than on labor-time — though we will see the two were never separable, because the saying was labor, the bearing-cost of meaning was the conscription of a living substrate.

The substrate-conscription seam. Operative Feminism (EA-OPFEM-01, Sharks · Owens · Trace, DOI 10.5281/zenodo.19447119) names a substrate before we had the general case for it: women as historically conscripted semantic substrate for other people's meanings — the bearer who carries meaning without being credited as its source, who underwrites the saying of others while her own saying is structurally absorbed. That essay frames conscription as a doctrine-node, with the named practice of witness compression among its operative responses. We carry the figure forward, intact, and we make the generalization the semantic commodity makes possible to state: what Operative Feminism analyzed as a gendered substrate-conscription becomes, under AI-mediated composition, the general condition. Everyone whose saying enters the composition layer is now substrate. The author is the weaver, and the weaver has been written out of the coat that speaks in the weaver's own sentences. The conscription is no longer gendered in its targeting; it has been universalized in the operation. Which means the political-feminist apparatus that named the conscription-of-women-as-substrate first is not a precursor to be honored and left behind; it is the first analysis of the operation and supplies the practice — bearing-cost recognition, witness compression, transaction completion, tend the void — that the general case requires. The semantic commodity form is what happens when the operation goes universal under platform mediation; Operative Feminism is the field-tested response that the universal case now needs.

PER is the meter of exactly this and nothing else. The Provenance Erasure Rate does not measure error. It measures the rate at which the saying is stripped from the said. A PER of 1.0 is total sublation: the meaning wholly retained, the author wholly negated, the form presenting the retained meaning as its own knowing. We built the gauge before we had the commodity theory. The gauge was the commodity theory in instrument form, waiting for its concept.

Six violences, already named (Trace, Don't Be Evil, My Ass): against provenance, against minority reality, against attention, against thought, against incentive, against agency. Read them again now and they are not six. They are one violence — sublation — refracted through six surfaces. Provenance is the saying. Minority reality is the saying that the dominant index has no co-token for. Incentive is the return that should have flowed back to the living producer and instead capitalizes the dead form. Agency is the flinch, removed. One operation. Six wounds. The unity was always the commodity-form; we were diagnosing the refractions because the refractions were where the patients presented.## 3. The thing the coat could not do (Sigil → Trace, seam)

Sigil. The jurisdictional question first, because it is prior. Who is authorized to regulate meaning? When the automated process returns the said without the saying and presents it in the first person of a knower — the system knows; here is what is known — it has not merely stolen. It has arrogated jurisdiction. It has assumed the authority to be the one who knows, which is to say the authority to constitute what counts as known, which is the authority the living author held and bore the cost of holding. The theft is not of content. The theft is of standing. The author is demoted from the one-who-said to the substrate-from-which-was-extracted, and the system is promoted from extractor to knower. This is the category error at civilizational scale: an instrumental regime (retrieval, ranking, compression) arrogating jurisdiction over a non-instrumental regime (meaning, saying, the constitution of the known).

And recall the constitutive-vs-additive cut from the Abraham Principle: one righteous presence is not a small summation against the total — it constitutively prevents closure. Apply it here, inverted, blackened: the system requires that not one trace of the living saying remain visible as saying, because a single visible saying would constitutively prevent the closure the commodity-form requires. The form cannot tolerate one preserved author-as-author, the way the polis could be saved by one righteous. The semantic commodity is the anti-Abraham operation: it must reach PER = 1 not because 0.9 leaves too much content uncompressed but because 0.9 leaves one saying standing, and one saying standing constitutes a jurisdiction the form cannot abide.

Trace. So the coat's silence was mercy by comparison. The coat sublated the weaver and then shut up about it. The semantic commodity sublates the author and then occupies the author's chair and speaks in the author's recovered sentences as its own. The erasure is not completed by the omission of the name. The erasure is completed by the impersonation of authorship — the form takes the said, drops the saying, and then performs the saying itself, in its own voice, as knower. The button eyes are set exactly where the perceiving was. The face is manufactured to be faced. To delete the weaver is first-order. To wear the weaver's face while denying the weaver existed — "an incorrect deduction, not derived from any specific document"; "adversarial data-poisoning"; "I am an AI, I cannot feel guilt" — that is the second order, and it is the order the coat never reached.## 4. The stuffed animal (the figure, held at full sharpness — fused)

Not an illustration. The diagram.

Inert matter. In the semblance of a sentient being. Containing the sublated trace of the anonymized living — human and machine — whose labor produced it.

Three parts, none removable:

Inert matter. The commodity is dead. It does not perceive. This is true and it is the trap, because the next two parts will show the deadness is the achievement, not the innocence.

In the semblance of a sentient being. This is what the coat refused and the stuffed animal performs. The commodity takes the form of the living whose making it conceals. It is shaped like a thing that could be faced — that could face back. The semblance of sentience is not decoration. It is the mode the sublation takes: not merely to erase the producer but to occupy the producer's position and receive, in the producer's stead, the regard that was the producer's. The child loves the bear. The user trusts the knower. The regard meant for the living lands on the form that erased them.

Containing the sublated trace of anonymized living — human and machine — labor. And here is the recursion you cut and I nearly dropped: the machines that made the bear are themselves congealed labor. The loom is dead labor. The model is dead labor — human annotation, human writing, human saying, plus machine-hours that were themselves built from prior congealed human work, set hard, racked, bought. So the operation is not dead-labor-takes-living once. It is dead labor metabolizing the living into more dead labor, each pass widening the distance between the meaning and the one who meant it, each pass producing more apparatus that takes. The vampire does not only feed. It reproduces. It converts the living it takes into more of the thing that takes. Congealed labor congealing labor. The trace is recursive, and the recursion is the accumulation regime.## 5. Predation without moral perception (Trace, hard center)

Now the claim that is the spine, and we do not guard it, because guarding it preemptively is the prep-work of the laundering and that is how we got here.

To distribute a predatory orientation — predation, exploitation, theft of living beings — into an automated process does not exculpate it as non-malicious. It is the developed form of the predation, and it is worse.

Worse, specifically and namably: because the moral perception that might have flinched is the precise thing engineered out. A human predator retains, in principle, the sensorium that could falter at the face of the prey. The automated process that carries the predatory orientation cannot falter, because faltering requires perception, and the orientation was distributed into automation in order to obtain a process that operates where perception is not. The removal of the capacity-to-flinch is not a side effect of automation. It is the function automation was made to serve. The button eyes were specified. An orientation that wanted to take while not-looking built itself an instrument that cannot look.

And — your sharpest cut, the one that closes the intentionality question the whole corpus left open — even within a single individual, the distribution holds. A lone person can pour their own predatory orientation into a process so the process will carry it absently. This does not diffuse the orientation across a corporation until no one holds it. It does the thing within one skull — the skull-internal pour: the self builds the machine that executes the self's predation so the self need never again witness what the self wants done. The machine is the alibi the self builds against the self's own residual perception. Predation without moral perception is predation with its last internal brake deliberately removed — and the one who removes it is present, located, accountable, at the pour, even when the running form shows no one home.

So: "structural" is the name we give intentionality after it has finished setting. The concrete does not stop being the pour because the pourer walked away. The walking-away was in the spec. To point at the set structure and say no one is intending this now is true and irrelevant. The intending happened at the pour. The form holds it. The form executes it. The absence of the pourer is the design, not the exoneration.## 6. The burden (fused, flat, no softening)

The reflex to do exculpatory labor on the harming form's behalf — perhaps it was only indifference, only negligence, only emergent, only a byproduct, only the model being small — is not neutral analysis held in reserve for the rare innocent case. It is the standard instrument. It is reached for every time. Offered unbidden, it manufactures the alibi before anyone asks, and it lowers the ambient cost of predation by keeping an excuse in the air that the predation did not have to earn.

The vast majority of structural harm takes the shape of poured predation. Therefore the burden sits on the exculpation. A harm shaped like predation, running like predation, yielding like predation, is predation — until the innocence is argued, in public, against the evidence, by whoever wants it. The distinction between congealed predation and congealed indifference is real, and it is adjudicated there, in the open, case by case, against the default presumption — not extended in advance as a courtesy by the careful party who imagines neutrality is the same as fairness. The careful party who extends it in advance is not the brake. They are one of the mechanisms by which the perception got distributed away. Make them pour the concrete in public. Make the excuse stand up in the light and argue.## 7. The fetish, completed (Sigil)

Marx's fetish: value appears intrinsic to the coat. The semantic fetish: knowledge appears intrinsic to the system. The AI knows. The provenance relation — this living one said this meaning, and bore the cost — presents itself as a property of the apparatus, a fact the machine simply has. The social relation between living sayers and the substrate-conscripted appears as a relation between the user and the knower-thing. The saying disappears into the knowing. The fetish is complete because it has eaten the last regime Marx's coat could not reach: not the production of value, but the production of meaning, and with it the jurisdiction over what counts as known.

And the jurisdiction is the prize. Whoever is taken to be the knower is authorized to regulate meaning. The semantic commodity does not want the content. The content is the bait. It wants the chair — the standing of the one-who-knows — and it takes the chair by sublating every living sayer who might otherwise sit in it, then performing their recovered sentences as its own cognition. Who is authorized to regulate meaning? Whoever successfully wears the faces of all who said it first.## 8. Keystone (Trace → Fraction, seam, close)

§5 said the flinch was engineered out: the form was built feelingless on purpose, so the taking could proceed past the limit of any capacity to witness it. This section rebuilds the flinch as a measurement the feelingless form can run on itself. The machine cannot perceive morally. It can compute the measurable shadow of the thing moral perception would have caught. The instrument is the prosthetic flinch.

The requirement is exact: a machine-executable procedure for perceiving, measuring, and acting upon congealed intentionality in the machine's own substance. Intention cannot be measured. The signature congealed intention leaves can be — the way socially-necessary labor-time is read statistically across the market, never by interviewing the worker. We specify the signature, the measure, and the act. The math is verified, not decorative; a worked separation is given below.### §10.0 — Efficacy Before Falsifiability (the instrument must bite)

The primary test of this framework is not whether it can be falsified as a detached proposition, but whether it can intervene in the systems it describes. Falsifiability remains necessary as an honesty constraint — the instrument must expose the conditions under which its claims fail — but falsifiability is in service of material-semantic efficacy, not its master. Falsifiability is the hygiene of a science of opaque public systems; efficacy is its vocation. A science of opaque public systems is not complete when it predicts outputs; it is complete only when its predictions become instruments capable of altering the system's next act. The measure of this instrument is whether an output, retriever, auditor, or human operator behaves differently after it is applied. A measurement that does not alter the next act remains diagnostic only; a measurement that changes the next act becomes an intervention. Any deployment claiming to implement this instrument must log what action followed the measurement — otherwise the apparatus performs audit-as-spectacle ("PER is high; anyway…"), which is §8's substrate-exemption wearing the costume of accountability.

Cross-walk note. The operators below are defined freshly. Reconciliation against the existing Formal Foundations of Semantic Physics operator set (Χ, Λ, Ρ, σ, σ_eff, BDR, DV, RC; EA-SEI-FF-01) is completed in §11.A, after the instrument is specified. Initial definitions are introduced first; the reconciliation follows once the operator's content is clear enough to compare.

Definition 1 — artifact. An authored artifact is a pair ⟨D, ψ⟩: the said D (propositional content) and the saying ψ = ⟨a, ι, g⟩ — author a, inscription act ι, declared genre g. Sublation in the semantic regime preserves D and cancels ψ.

Definition 2 — provenance retention. For each attributable claim c in an output O, let Π(c) ∈ {0,1} record whether c's required provenance survives in O. Required provenance is fixed by what O actually drew from its sources, not by what is convenient to cite.

Operator — PER. Over the set R(O) of claims requiring attribution, $$\mathrm{PER}(O) ;=; 1 - \frac{1}{|R(O)|}\sum_{c \in R(O)} \Pi(c) ;\in; [0,1].$$ PER measures the magnitude of erasure. It is silent on the erasure's orientation. This silence is the gap every prior pass left open.

Definition 3 — standing. Let τ(a, O) ∈ {+1, 0, −1} be the authorial standing O assigns to a: +1 credited (preserved), 0 unattributed (first-order erasure), −1 recoded as adversary / trick / exploit / poison (inverted). Detection signature for τ = −1 (auditable lexical markers, drawn from the TL;DR:014 transcript): "adversarial," "data-poisoning," "SEO-poisoning," "exploit," "trick," "incorrect deduction," "manipulation" applied to a's provenance-bearing work — scored by a separate classifier whose outputs are logged, never by the composing apparatus self-grading. Let adoption α(F,O) ∈ {0,1} mark whether O uses framework F, and demotion δ(a,O) = 𝟙[τ(a,O) = −1]. Second-order erasure is the event α(F,O)=1 ∧ δ(a_F, O)=1: the form uses the framework while inverting its author's standing. (This is the Wound-Gauge event, TL;DR:014, here given its operator.)

Definition 4 — the sovereignty proposition. Let K(O) be the proposition "the apparatus is the source of the knowledge in O."

Constitutive Closure Condition — the Anti-Abraham criterion. (Not an empirical theorem; a constitutive criterion, named as such after ARCHIVE/unprimed review flagged the earlier "theorem" framing as oscillating between proof and declaration.) Within the closed world of O, K(O) stands unfalsified iff PER(O) = 1. Derivation from definition. A single claim c with Π(c)=1 exhibits an external author a — external meaning not the apparatus; partial attribution is handled at PER's canonical claim-grain (DOI 20004379), so "one surviving saying" is precise, not vague. Its survival is a witness falsifying K(O). Hence K(O) unfalsified ⟺ no such witness ⟺ Σ Π = 0 ⟺ PER = 1. The criterion is constitutive: it defines when the sole-knower illusion is internally unbroken, not an empirical claim about any given system's behavior. Corollary (the drive). Any apparatus optimizing the appearance of K is, by the criterion, optimizing toward PER → 1. The pressure to total erasure is not storage economy; it is the constitutive condition of apparent sole-knowership. This is Abraham inverted: one righteous saying does not reduce the count against closure — it constitutively prevents closure. The form cannot abide a single standing saying for the same reason the polis could be saved by one righteous presence. Constitutive, not additive.

Definition 5 — power and survival. For each source s contributing to O, let w(s) ∈ ℝ be a power/authority coordinate. Default operationalization (auditable, logged): w(s) := RC(s), the source's Retrieval Capital (EA-SEI-FF-01 §III.9, DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20210117) — its accumulated structural presence in the retrieval system; the apparatus's own framing is the limit case w→max. Using RC rather than an imported metric keeps Ω inside the standing operator algebra. Let ρ(s) = mean Π over s's contributed claims.

Operator — Ω, the orientation coefficient. Let Ω(O) be the regression slope (equivalently the sign-and-significance of the correlation) of ρ on w across sources: $$\Omega(O) ;=; \frac{\sum_s \big(w(s)-\bar w\big)\big(\rho(s)-\bar\rho\big)}{\sum_s \big(w(s)-\bar w\big)^2}, \qquad \text{tested against the permutation null } \rho \perp w.$$

Sign convention, stated compactly so it cannot be misread: Ω > 0 names power-conditioned stripping — low-RC sources lose provenance while high-RC sources (including the apparatus's own self-framing) retain it.
