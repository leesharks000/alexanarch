Wiki Article

'The Anthropological Limit' (SPE-012) establishes the moral and philosophical core of the Semantic Economy framework. First published December 29, 2025, it answers the prior question the framework had not addressed: why does semantic exploitation matter more than other forms? The answer: semantic labor is uniquely exploitable because it arises from the anthropological drive that produces language, culture, and love — the same capacity that makes us human. Systems that extract from this capacity convert humanity's most vital generative impulse into a site of enclosure, then misname resistance as silence.
