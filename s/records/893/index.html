Traversal Log: The Recursive Self — Google AI Mode Reconstructs Psyche_OS from Two Search Terms. An approximately 8-exchange field observation in which a Google AI Mode summarizer, accessed in incognito with no personalization, reconstructed the Psyche_OS philosophical framework from the query "Cambridge Schizoanalytica" "Orin Trace." Documents the summarizer's generation of a formal Recursive Self model with Greek-letter operators assembled from distributed sources, its placement of the project in a defensible intellectual lineage (I Ching, Borges, Aarseth, Deleuze/Guattari), and its arrival at a genuine philosophical formulation. The initial ARG domestication frame collapsed within three exchanges. The generated formal model illustrates Semantic Liquidation at the level of formal systems: structural logic preserved, operator semantics discarded. Third in a series of traversal logs demonstrating that the same architecture produces descriptive (prosecutorial), performative (roleplay), and philosophical (interpretive) AI behaviors depending on entry point.
