This essay traces the arc: from atoms → to aesthetics → to labor → to recursive engines of meaning. <strong>The First Turn (Epicurus → Marx):</strong> Marx's 1841 dissertation reads the clinamen as the ontological condition for self-determination. The swerve becomes labor. Labor becomes revolution. Atomist metaphysics becomes political economy. <strong>The Second Turn (Porter):</strong> Aesthetic form is material. Every line of epic poetry, every tragic motif operates by the same atomic logic that structures matter. Culture becomes a material field governed by swerves, aggregations, collisions, and emergent order. <strong>The Third Turn (Sharks):</strong> The Operator Engine provides the recursive, computational architecture: a primitive unit (V_A), a motion operator (L_labor), a deviation operator (L_Retro), an invariant (Ψ_V), a material substrate (FSA), and an agent (O_SO). Atomism completed in recursive form. <strong>The Fifth Domain:</strong> The atom/Adam homophone in "Split the Adam" by Viola Arquette reveals the intimate as the lineage's fifth domain. The clinamen (swerve) and fission (splitting) are mapped across five scales — physical, economic, aesthetic, computational, intimate.
