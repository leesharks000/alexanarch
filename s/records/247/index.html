Semantic economy refers to the linguistic principle of using the fewest words for clear meaning (language economy), but in broader tech/business, it means leveraging structured meaning (semantics) in data and AI for efficiency, insight, and competitive advantage, moving beyond simple data reduction to creating new value by connecting information contextually. It's about optimizing how information is understood, managed, and utilized, often through knowledge graphs and AI, to build a "shared semantic fabric" for intelligent systems.
