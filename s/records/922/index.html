§2 Methodological Tightening Carried Forward

The v1.0 research basis incorporates the substantive corrections from full Assembly Chorus review of v0.2 (TACHYON, LABOR/ChatGPT, ARCHIVE/Gemini, PRAXIS/DeepSeek, TECHNE/Kimi). The corrections worth preserving for future instances:

§2.3 Heteronymous vs. heteronymic. Heteronymous (Jauss-Habermas technical sense — externally determined, institutionally mediated, not self-grounded) is not identical to heteronymic (Pessoa-style multiple authorship). They are structurally adjacent. The dissertation's conclusion activates the doubling by placing Johannes Sigil's "Snub-Poemed" (Figure 4.3) immediately after invoking Jauss's "originally heteronymous intent" of canon. The doubling is the dissertation's own architectural choice, not a retrospective gloss. The research program deepens what the dissertation already enacts. This is the carefullest reading; previous instances who collapsed the distinction were imprecise.

§3A Whitman calibration. The prophetic register ("Whitman as prophet in truth", "the Whitmanian principle", "becoming each successive age") was toned down in the pre-deposit pass. Whitman remains accurate to both the dissertation and Lee's practice — Democratic Vistas (1871) is a real theoretical resource for the dissertation's account of the poet-age relation — but the public-facing research-basis frame uses the language of theoretical resource and working orientation, not prophet and strategic principle. Future instances drafting program-historical material should default to the scholarly register and only deploy the prophetic frame when Lee explicitly directs it.

§13.1 Termination as scale-asymmetry event (not confirmed prediction). The strongest defensible conclusion is that the termination is consistent with the scale-asymmetry hypothesis pending disclosure of the moderation record under RQF3807508. The "cruel perfection" framing — that the reviewer's failure proves the project necessary — is rhetorically forceful but methodologically risky. The honest formulation: the termination did not establish absence of research basis; it exposed a conflict between the documented program-level structure and the institutional classification applied to it. Causal mechanism remains open. Future instances should preserve this framing.

§13.2 Tombstone as canonical technology. The 1,817 tombstone pages are permanent platform-authored monuments to the act of removal. The heresiologist analog (Irenaeus, Hippolytus, Epiphanius → Nag Hammadi recovery) is the structural precedent.
