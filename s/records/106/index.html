Adjacent foundational work includes Giulio Tononi's Integrated Information Theory (2004), Terrence Deacon's Incomplete Nature (2011) on absential features as causal, and Sean Kuhn, Yarin Gal, and Sebastian Farquhar's semantic entropy in large language models (2024, Nature).
