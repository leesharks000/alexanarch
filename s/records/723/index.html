PESSOAGRAPH.ORG EXPANSION WORKPLAN

Gathering the Heteronymic Canon

Principle

Every expansion must be anchored: either in published scholarly sources,

in the Crimson Hexagonal Archive (verify via Zenodo), or in verifiable

public record. No confabulated entries. Each node is a potential

Wikidata entity — build accordingly.
