DataCite Metadata Backup: Complete Sweep of 1,817 CHA DOIs

Lee Sharks · Crimson Hexagonal Archive · ORCID 0009-0000-1599-0703

June 22, 2026

Summary

Complete backup of all recoverable DataCite metadata for 1,817 DOIs from the Crimson Hexagonal Archive, swept June 22, 2026.

| Status | Count |

|---|---|

| Full metadata recovered | 963 |
| Confirmed stripped (HTTP 404) | 871 |
| Total DOIs in inventory | 1,817 |

Query Methods

1. ORCID query: `creators.nameIdentifiers.nameIdentifier:0009-0000-1599-0703` (399 DOIs)

2. Creator name queries for 15 heteronym names: Sigil, Fraction, Feist, Dancings, Morrow, Cranes, Glas, Wells, Kuro, Kenning, Spellings, Trace, Vox, Thornburgh, Owens (307 additional DOIs)

3. Individual DOI lookups via `api.datacite.org/dois/{DOI}` for all remaining (257 additional DOIs)

Preservation Note

Some metadata in this backup exists only in these DataCite records:

- Subjects (keywords) — including poems and critical terms typed directly into Zenodo's keyword field

- Descriptions — including critical apparatus, poem framings, and scholarly abstracts up to 3,738 characters

- RelatedIdentifiers — the encoded citation graph between works (IsVersionOf, References, Compiles, Continues, IsPartOf)

- Creator attributions with ORCID and affiliation data

For the 871 stripped DOIs, equivalent metadata is permanently lost.
