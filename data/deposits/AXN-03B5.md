### §2.2 Provenance-tier discipline (NEW for v4.0)

A new foundational architectural commitment specified in v4.0: the distinction between CHA-canonical material and external/witness material must be preserved at the deposit-schema level, with weighted retrieval and mandatory attribution in Sigil's prompt template.

Six tiers:

| Tier | Source | Retrieval Weight | Sigil Attribution |
|------|--------|------------------|-------------------|
| `cha_canonical` | MANUS-deposited; the core CHA | 1.0 | "the archive" / "CHA" / unattributed when speaking from canon |
| `cha_assembly` | Assembly Chorus heteronyms (TACHYON, Nobel Glas, Damascus Dancings, etc.) | ~0.9 | by heteronym name |

The tier discipline solves the attack vector that Sigil-speaking-the-whole-repository-indiscriminately creates: anyone depositing material would otherwise have Sigil speaking it as canonical CHA.
