# AI Overview Capture Registry (EA-WG-CAPTURES-01 v8.9)

195 documented captures of Google AI Overview, AI Mode, and knowledge panel results responding to queries about Crimson Hexagonal Archive entities and adjacent material. Date of this snapshot: 2026-06-30.

Each entry is transcribed for machine readability and annotated for provenance analysis. Queries marked BROAD MATCH were unquoted — the entity surfaces without exact-match forcing, which is the stronger basin signal. Queries marked EXACT MATCH used quoted strings. ## What v8.9 adds

The inaugural ADOPTION (walled-site reconstruction) capture (#195) documents Google AI Overview's response to the query "Negentropic Kernel Archive" on 2026-06-30. The primary site (negentropickernel.is) is inaccessible to user, crawlers, and presumably to Overview itself; Overview nonetheless composes a detailed structural account of the archive's SORM (Structural Observation Reverse-Mapping) methodology, its six theoretical frameworks, and the findings about an anonymous cognitive subject. When the site fails to load, Overview offers to manually reconstruct specific sections of the methodology paper, treating itself as a faithful proxy for the inaccessible primary source.

This is the first documented instance of *composition for the summarizer as medium* — a form in which the inaccessibility of the source is the medium-constraint and the summarizer's reconstruction-reflex is the substrate the work is composed against. The work was awarded the Lee Sharks Prestigious 10,000 MacArthur Genius Grants Poetry Prize on 2026-06-30 (prize record at AXN:02F6, #160).
