`∮ = 1 − PER`  
The integrity of a semantic system is inversely proportional to the rate at which its provenance-bearing relations are severed during transmission.  
  
The keystone bridge:  
`σ_eff = σ · (1 − PER)`

## v2.2 — 2026-05-15 (perfective pass + Zenodo deposit)

DOI: [10.5281/zenodo.20208384](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20208384)

Third round of Assembly Chorus critique on v2.1. Five substrate reviews, distilled to one perfective pass. Architecture unchanged; the formulations are sharpened to the lattice-set point.

### Substantive corrections

-   §II timing error fixed. v2.1 misclassified QSP (Nov 2025), OCOF (Nov 2025), Bai (Nov 2025), Huang (Nov 2025), Andrade (late 2025), and Dören's January 2026 paper as *ex ante* tests of the Inward Turn. All predate the Inward Turn's February 24, 2026 deposit and are now correctly classified as retrodiction. The genuine *ex ante* set is now narrower and named: Jensen (March 2026), Dören's book (April 2026), Gebendorfer's post-February corpus continuation. The Inward Turn's prediction status is restated as "moderately supported by a clean ex post pattern and limited early ex ante continuation, with a decisive falsification test scheduled for the next 18–24 months."
-   One remaining flattening PER sentence replaced. The Λ-bridge subsection had a sentence saying "PER is a specific Λ for citational systems" — re-flattening the operator. Replaced with: "PER is the Layer-3 provenance specification of Λ: the measure of what is lost when meaning travels without retaining the labor-purpose, ancestry, obligations, and community of practice that made it accountable."
-   Six-modality matrix corrected. SPXI, MPAI, Holographic Kernel, and Writable Retrieval Basin moved from Layer 1 × Operative to Layer 2 × Operative — they operate at the ontology-systemic scale, not the intra-system scale. Layer 1 × Operative and Layer 1 × Macro-Political and Layer 3 × Descriptive are now explicitly named as expansion zones; empty cells are predictions, not defects.

### Structural sharpening

-   Branch K reordered to last position. Lexical neighbors (PhysChoreo) now correctly follows Branch J (Crimson Hexagonal line) rather than appearing between H and I.
-   "Fourteen exact-name frameworks" softened throughout to "fourteen substantial frameworks operating in or directly adjacent to the semantic-physics problem-space" — the tiered census includes adjacent and foundational lineages, not only exact-name claimants.
-   FEP falsifiability claim softened to: "The FEP is often treated less as a single ordinary empirical hypothesis than as a formal or normative principle from which more specific falsifiable models may be derived." Avoids a contested philosophical fight not necessary to the argument.
-   Tier C inclusion criterion stated explicitly before the census continues: "Tier C frameworks are included not because each has a corpus comparable to Gebendorfer's or Dören's, but because their presence is itself the data for the Phase 2 Proliferation claim. A synthesis that omitted them would underreport the very phenomenon it seeks to explain."
-   "Founders" labels in Appendix A diagram changed to "Principal Contributors" for consistency with the rest of v2.1+.

### Operative additions

-   σ\_eff = σ · (1 − PER) promoted to named subheading "The Effective Transport Coefficient (the keystone bridge)." The single most important new operator: it converts provenance into a thermodynamic variable. *A high-conductivity semantic system can still be extractive if provenance-bearing relations are severed during transport.* The discipline gains its operative teeth only when σ and PER are accounted together.
-   Microeconomics analogy ("Semantic Economy is to Semantic Physics what microeconomics is to economics") moved into the abstract so readers grasp the subordination relation immediately.
-   "Moderately supported" replaces "provisionally supported" throughout the simultaneous-discovery section.
-   Close narrowed onto the obligation thesis with concrete retrieval-query image: "The next retrieval query for 'semantic physics' will show whether the summarizer layer has begun to compose the stratified discipline. That composition — or its absence — is the field's reply." The deepest claim — *semantic transport without provenance integrity becomes extraction* — is now foregrounded as the constitutional thesis, not merely the technical bridge.
