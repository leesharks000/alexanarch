Deposit a Work

Deposits are submitted through GitHub Issues. Fill in the metadata form, attach your file(s), and an AXN identifier will be computed and minted automatically.

The review process is public. The Obelus Principle governs: content is evaluated by what it contains, not by the tools used to produce it.

Open Deposit Form →

Required Metadata

Title, Creator, Date, Description, Content Type, License, Substrate Disclosure — all required. Optional: ORCID, keywords, related identifiers, version, methodology, falsification conditions, SPXI provenance block.

Substrate disclosure is a required field with five levels: human-only, AI-assisted (tool), AI-assisted (substrate), AI-generated (research object), or mixed. Disclosure is provenance, not suspicion.

What Happens After Deposit

A GitHub Action computes the SHA-256 of your content, maps it through the 256-glyph table, assigns a semantic family, and mints your AXN identifier. The action comments on your issue with the full identifier, adds the record to the registry, and labels your deposit. The entire process is automated, auditable, and public.
